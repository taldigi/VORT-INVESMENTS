About Vort Investments.

Vort Investments is UK based and operated but with a “Global Focus” and will therefore consider investment opportunities all kind as our “Global Network” of client’s, partners, connections, and support is spanning in 15 countries and territories, with a team of 25 talented financial service specialists based in the United Kingdom and 35 professionals representative’s across the United States, Switzerland, Germany, Belgium, Netherland, Spain, India, Japan, Hong Kong, Singapore, China, Australia, and New Zealand.

The establishment of the Vort Investments started in 2017, but the actual company was formed in 2020 as the on-going preparation took 3 years’ of time as each member of our team has its own “Sophisticated Network” of clients in different sectors and “Connections” within the industry to accelerate the potential and possibilities of success, and our goal was to “Put Everything into One Pot and Become One” as to gather the wealth, and knowledge of everyone together which we have successfully managed. In total, we look after around £65 million for our clients (figures as of 2021).

We provide an opportunity for private investors, venture capitalists, and family offices to access unique investment opportunities that match their specific investment interests.

We use our extensive network in Europe, the USA, and Asia to provide our exclusive network of investors with exciting investment opportunities across multiple markets. In addition to pre-screening the investment opportunities, we only pass on opportunities that could match your investment criteria.

Accuracy, quality of service, transparency, and conscientiousness are just a few of the high expectation, we provide you with relevant information in a consistent format so that you can quickly and easily evaluate the investment opportunity. We understand your professional requirements.

Vort Investments provides a professional environment that ensures that parties are only introduced once the confidentiality of their discussions and information exchanged is ensured.
