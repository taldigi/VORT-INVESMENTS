About us

Vort Investments Limited is a young and ambitious Global Private Wealth management firm with a passion for raising “Smart Capital” for premium private well-established SMEs, as well as early-stage and growth-stage start-ups.

Founded in 2020 in the UK by a highly experienced team which includes a combination of mixed industry high net-worth individuals, entrepreneurs, and government individuals, many of whom have held senior roles in business as well as wealth management experts, project managers, and investment professionals with an accumulated financial business background of over 100 years.

Mainly focused on elderly clients aged between 60 or over as well as high net- worth families, SME business owners who are actively seeking to preserve their wealth but have neither the time nor the inclination, to manage themselves or prefer to stay in the background.
